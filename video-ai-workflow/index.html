AI Video Creation

From Story to Video with AI

Welcome!

- Today we'll learn a complete workflow for creating AI-generated videos
- This process combines storytelling with AI tools
- By the end, you'll understand each step to create your own AI videos
STEP 1

Create a Role

üé≠

Define who is telling the story. Is it a narrator, a character, a brand voice, or a documentary style? The role shapes the entire tone.

Step 1: Creating a Role

- This is your foundation - who is speaking?
- Examples: A wise narrator, an excited character, a calm teacher
- The role influences word choice, pacing, and emotional tone
- Think about your audience - what role resonates with them?
STEP 2

Think the Story

üí≠

Brainstorm your narrative. What's the beginning, middle, and end? What emotion do you want to evoke? What's the key message?

Step 2: Thinking the Story

- Don't write yet - just think and visualize
- What scenes do you see in your mind?
- What emotion should viewers feel at the end?
- Keep it simple - complex stories are hard to execute in AI video
STEP 3

Tell Story to ChatGPT

ü§ñ

Share your story idea and purpose with ChatGPT. Explain the role, the narrative, and what you're trying to achieve with the video.

Step 3: Communicating with ChatGPT

- Be conversational - explain like you're telling a friend
- Include: Who is the role? What's the story? Why this video?
- ChatGPT will help structure and refine your ideas
- This is collaborative - iterate back and forth
STEP 4

Prepare the Prompt

üìù

Work with ChatGPT to create detailed prompts for each scene. These prompts will be used to generate images in the next step.

Scene-by-Scene Visual Details Style Consistency Lighting/Mood Character Details
Step 4: Preparing the Prompts

- Break your story into individual scenes
- Each scene needs a detailed image prompt
- Include: Subject, setting, lighting, style, mood
- Keep style consistent across all prompts
- ChatGPT can help write these prompts
STEP 5

Generate Images

üé®

Use AI image generation tools to create visuals for each scene. Generate multiple variations and select the best ones.

Step 5: Generating Images

- Use your prepared prompts to generate images
- Generate multiple versions - pick the best
- Maintain visual consistency across scenes
- Tools: DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, etc.
STEP 6

Input to Video AI

üé¨

Feed your generated images into a Video AI tool to bring them to life. Add motion, transitions, and bring your story into video format.

Step 6: Video AI Input

- Upload your images to a video AI tool
- These tools add motion and life to static images
- Popular tools: Runway, Pika Labs, Kling AI
- Specify motion type: pan, zoom, character movement
STEP 7

Make Corrections

‚ú®

Review the generated video and make refinements. Adjust timing, regenerate problematic clips, and polish until it's perfect.

Review Each Clip Adjust Timing Regenerate Issues Add Audio/Music Final Polish
Step 7: Making Corrections

- Watch the video critically
- Identify clips that don't work well
- Regenerate with adjusted prompts if needed
- Add voiceover, music, sound effects
- This step may take multiple iterations
